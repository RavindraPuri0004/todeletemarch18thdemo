Course Overview

Full Stack Application Development with .NET and Node is comprehensive program to give you the complete experience of software development with the respective technologies. The course involves training on Front-end technologies such as - HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, .NET technologies – C# and ASP.NET, and MEAN Stack – Mongo DB, Angular, Node and Express JS.

  • No standing arrears in current education

Need Assistance

FAQs

Will placement assistance be provided?

Yes, the participants will be provided placement assistance by L&T Edutech and ASAP.

What will be the mode of course delivery?

The sessions will be held online.

On what platform will the sessions be delivered?

The LMS platform of L&T Edutech will be used to deliver the sessions.

What are the placement eligibility criteria?
  • 2020 & 2021 pass out students of B.E/B.Tech./ME/M.Tech (CS/ IT/ Circuit Branches of Engineering), MCA (full-time/ regular courses) – can be looked at for immediate placement
  • Students in the last year of other courses like BE/BTech/ ME/ MTech (CS/IT/Circuit Branches of Engineering), MCA, BSc CS, BCA – can be offered the course but won’t be eligible for internal hiring.
What are the class timings?

Out of 400 hours, 156 hours are live instructor-led sessions. This will be conducted six hours a week (the timing of these sessions will be determined based on the convenience of the learners).
